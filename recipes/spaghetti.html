Spaghetti Recipe

Spaghetti is a classic dish for people who are poor and can't afford anything else to eat. Here's how to make this sad sad meal.

You'll need:

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • A can of tomato sauce
  • Meat (if available)

Directions:

  1. Boil Noodles
  2. While noodles are boiling, heat up sauce
  3. When noodles are done, pour sauce over top.
  4. Add meat i available.
